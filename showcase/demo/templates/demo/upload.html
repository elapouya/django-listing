Upload

You can upload images with drag & drop, django-listing includes views to manage upload and actions, the setup is very simple

It uses Dropzone.js, django-listing automatically add dropzone CSS and JS. Urls can be customized in settings.py

Have a look at demo/templates/demo/upload.html, UploadListingView in demo/views.py and UploadListing in demo/listings.py to see how examples are coded
